BAMSI

Bamsi Beyrek (Azerbaijani: Bamsı Beyrək, South Azerbaijani: بامسی بئیرک, Turkish: Bamsı Beyrek) is a character in the Book of Dede Korkut and in Turkish, Azeri and some Altai legends and fairy tales. Despite his stories being far-stretched, it is believed that he may have indeed been a real person. This is a list of characters from the Turkish TV series Diriliş: Ertuğrul, created by Mehmet Bozdağ. It focuses on the life of Ertuğrul, father of Osman I, founder of the Ottoman Empire. In the series, Ertuğrul is portrayed by Engin Altan Düzyatan and Osman is portrayed by Emre Üçtepe. Most of the notable characters in Diriliş: Ertuğrul are based on real people related to Osman I and the founding of the Ottoman Empire, including Osman I himself, Halime Hatun and Ertuğrul, or fictional characters adapted from the Book of Dede Korkut, such as Selcan Hatun, Banu Çiçek and Bamsı Beyrek. Most of the characters however, were created by the producers of the series as there were just not that many sources from the era.[1] Every season has additional cast members starring with a main role.
